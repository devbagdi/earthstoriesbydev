My Research Interests

Exploring Anthropogenic-Mediated Change in Natural Ecosystems

In my research, I delve into the intricate web of anthropogenic-mediated changes in natural ecosystems. Specifically, I am intrigued by the multifaceted pressures imposed by the existence and growth of Homo sapiens. From alterations in biodiversity to shifts in ecological dynamics, my focus extends to understanding how these ecosystems adapt, cope, and, importantly, how we can actively contribute to their preservation and restoration.
