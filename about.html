About Dev

Where is Dev? Who is Dev? Why is Dev?

Hello, I'm Dev Bagdi, an emerging ecologist who thrives in the great outdoors. My passion lies in contemplating how we can enhance the world for both humans and every living being. A dedicated storyteller, I capture the beauty of nature through my lens, using photography as a medium to convey the stories that unfold around me.

Immersed in the wilderness, I ponder ways to contribute to a better world. Through my photography and storytelling, I aspire to inspire others to appreciate and protect the delicate balance of our ecosystems. Join me on this journey where each image and story is a step towards creating a more sustainable and compassionate planet for us all.
